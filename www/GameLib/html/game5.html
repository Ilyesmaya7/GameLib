Mr. Mail is here to help! Come sleet or hail or magical items, help these adventurers pack their bags and receive fabulous treasure!

Controls:

Left click to drag items
Hold left click and press A or D to rotate, scroll wheel to scale items (or W to scale up, S to scale down)
Hover over character icons on top left to switch between multiple bags
Grab star sweets from the jar in the bottom-left
Left click bell to finish packing!
Press T or Tab or \ for Dark Mode
